Yanfei

Yanfei is a 4 star character who is from Liyue. She is an adepti. Although she has not signed a contract with Rex Lapis, she devotes herself as a legal advisor to Liyue. She has a pyro vision and she is a mage. Her birthday is on July 28th.

Since she is a mage,she does well in good catalyst. I use her as my main DPS and for that I would best equip her with Tome Of The Eternal Flow If you're lucky enough to have Neuvillette's weapon in Genshin Impact, it can be well-suited for Yanfei since it already provides a massive 88.1 percent Crit DMG boost after being upgraded, which already takes away a lot of pressure from your artifact farming. Apart from that, this weapon can increase her Charged Attack DMG by up to 42 percent while also regenerating some Energy when you have full stacks of this Charged Attack DMG boost. Both of these effects work great with Yanfei since she needs her Burst to perform Charged Attacks faster.
